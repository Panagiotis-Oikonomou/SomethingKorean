그리고 conjunction

Description:

그리고 is a conjunction that means "and" or "and then", depending on the context.

Youtube link
You can use it just as 그리고 or the short version below.
Structure:
Present tense: Base stem + 고
Past tense: Past stem + 고
Future tense: Future stem + 고

Examples:
1. And: Connecting two nouns, phrases, or clauses

사과 그리고 바나나를 샀어요.
I bought apples and bananas.

저는 학생이고 제 친구는 선생님이에요.
I'm a student. And my friend is a teacher.
2. And then: Showing a sequence of actions

집에 갔어요. 그리고 잤어요.
I went home, and then I slept.

밥을 먹었고 영화를 봤어요.
I ate, and then I watched a movie.
