그러면, 그러려면 conjunctions

Description:

그러면 translates to "then" or "in that case" in English. It's often used when you're talking about what will happen next or suggesting a course of action, based on a previous statement. 그러려면 is a more specific form of 그러면, and it translates to "if you want to do that" in English, but with an added sense of a condition for achieving or doing something. It's used when you're talking about a condition or requirement that needs to be met in order to reach a certain goal or to take a particular action. It's like saying, "In order to do that" or "If you want that to happen".

You can use it just as 그러면/그러려면 or the short version below.

Structure:
Present tense: Present stem + (으)면
Present tense: Base stem + (으)려면

Examples:
많이 먹어 그러면 살이 쪄요.
If you eat a lot then you will gain weight.

A: 티켓 얼마예요?
     How much is the ticket?
B: 학생이면이면 1000원이고 성인이면 2000원이에요.
     If you are a student it's 1000won and if you are adult it's 2000won.

지갑을 찿으려면 어디에 신고해야 돼요?
To find my wallet where should I report it?

감기에 걸리지 않아 그러려면 자주 손을 씻어야 해요.
In order to not catch a cold you should wash your hands often.
