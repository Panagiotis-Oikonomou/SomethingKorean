-더라/더라고 Ending

Description:

-더라/더라고 are used as sentence-ending expressions to convey that you are sharing something you personally observed, experienced, or learned in the past. When you use the verb in the past tense it conveys the feeling of something you observed, experienced or learned in the past about the past.

Youtube link
Structure:
Verb/Adjective/이다 stem + 더라/더라고

Verb/Adjective/이다 + 았/었더라고

Examples:
이제 소풍 갔는데 날씨가 진짜 좋더라.
I went on a picnic yesterday and the weather was so nice.

아까 한국어 수업을 들었는데 어렵더라고요.
I took a Korean class earlier and it was difficult.

우리 이모가 죽었더라고요.
In the past I realized my aunt passed away.
