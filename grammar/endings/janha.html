-잖아 Ending

Description:

-잖아 is used for obvious facts on anything the other person already knows. You use it when trying to remind somebody of something or to prove a point by reminding something.

Youtube link
Structure:
Verb/Adjective/이다 stem + 잖아

Examples:
그 사람은 우리 학교 선생님이잖아.
That person is our school teacher, you know.

A: 너 이름이 뭐야?
     What is your name?
B: 아까 말하잖아요.
     I told you earlier right.

A: 왜 울어요?
     Why are you crying?
B: 너 때문이잖아요.
     It's beacause of you.
