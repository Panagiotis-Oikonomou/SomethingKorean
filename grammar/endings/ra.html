-라 Ending

Description:

-라 is used to form informal commands or imperatives — basically telling someone to do something in a casual and sometimes authoritative tone.

Youtube link
Structure:
Verb-아/어 + 라

Examples:
이웃을 보면 인사 좀 해라.
If you came across your neighboor greet them.

조용히 해라!
Be quiet!

이 책을 읽어라.
Read this book.

집에 가라.
Go home.
