-거든 Ending

Description:

-거든 is used to give a reason to what somebody said/asked or what was previously mentioned or what you are about to say right now (conversation opener, backup info for what you will say). The second way to use it is to strongly object to what someone else has said.

Youtube link
Structure:
Verb/Adjective/이다 stem + 거든

Examples:
A: 너 왜 늦어니?
     Why are you late?
B: 길이 막혔거든요.
     Because the road was blocked.

A: 지수가 지호랑 사귄대요.
     I heard Jisoo is dating Jiho.
B: 아니거든요.
     No she is not.
