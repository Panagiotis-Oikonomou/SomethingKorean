-다 Ending

Description:

-다 is used in written form (반말), spoken form (반말), interchangeably with -네 and in place of future tense (verb).

Youtube link
Structure:
Present tense
Verb stem-ㄴ/는다
Adjective stem + 다

Past tense
Verb/Adjective stem + 었다/았다

Future tense
Verb/Adjective-ㄹ/을 거다

Examples:
오늘은 날씨가 좋다.
Today, the weather is nice.

이 책은 재미있다.
This book is interesting.

내일 비가 온다 꼭 미리 우산 사요.
It's going to rain tomorrow so make sure you buy an umbrella in advance.
