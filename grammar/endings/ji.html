-지 Ending

Description:

-지 is used for something that the speaker considers obvious or thoughtful. It can be used regardless of whether the other person agrees with me. It can can also be used to suggest what to do.

Youtube link
Structure:
Verb/Adjective/이다 stem + 지

Examples:
A: 나 예뻐?
     Am I pretty?
B: 예쁘지.
     To me you are obvious pretty.

A: 나 그거 너무 비싸서 안 샀어 나중에 살래.
     It was too expensive so I didn't buy it. I will buy it tomorrow
B: 아 사죠 내일이 가격 더 오르거둔요.
     You should have bought it. Because the price will go higher tomorrow.
