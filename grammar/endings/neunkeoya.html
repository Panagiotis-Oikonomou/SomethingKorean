-거야 Ending

Description:

-거야 express explanation, emphasis, clarification, or even surprise.

Youtube link
Structure:
Verb/Adjective/이다 stem-ㄴ/는 거야

Examples:
(dramatic effect)나 어제 공원은 지나고 있었거든 근데 곧 개가 갑자기 나 한테 오더니 나를 무는 거야.
I was moving by the park but a big dog was running to me and bite me.

(dramatic effect)늦잠을 갔는데 지각을 했어 근데 선생님이 너무 심하게 혼내는 거야.
I overslept and didn't go to class and the teacher scold me.

A: (emphasize)나 80점 받았어 합격 못했어요
     I got 80 points. I didn't pass.
B: 합격 최저 점수가 70점이야. 너 합격한 거예요.
     The lowest passing score is 70. You passed.
