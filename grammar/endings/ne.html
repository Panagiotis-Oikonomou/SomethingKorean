-네 Ending

Description:

-네 is used to express surprise, realization, or an exclamation about something that the speaker has just noticed or discovered.

Youtube link 1   Youtube link 2
Structure:
Verb/Adjective stem + 네

Examples:
한국어를 잘하네요.
I can see that you are good at Korean.
경치가 정말 예쁘네요.
I can see that the view is really pretty.
지수씨는 정말 똑똑하네요.
I can see that Jisoo is really smart.
