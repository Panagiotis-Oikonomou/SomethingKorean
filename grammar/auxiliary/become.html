-지다 Auxiliary Verb

Description:

The -지다 auxiliary verb indicates a change in state or condition, similar to saying "to become" or "to get". It's often used to describe the process of something changing or becoming a certain way.

Youtube link
Structure:
Adjective-아/어 + 지다

Examples:
고추장을 넣으면 더 매워질 거예요.
If you put the red pepper paste it will become spycier.

한국어 실력이 예전보다 나아졌어요.
My Korean skills have become better from then.

갑자기 맥주를 마시고 싶어지네요.
I suddenly want to drink a beer.

이 책은 둬로 갈 수럭 재미있어져요.
As the book goes towards the end it becomes more fun.
When you use -고 싶다, you didn't want to do it before but now you want.
