Verb/Adjective-았/었던

It can only mean this two:

(1) Was the case in the past and not anymore (past state affairs).

(2) Past event or actions that ended or was completed.

Youtube link
(1) 여렸을 때 아주 작았던 지수가 지금 이렇게 컸네요.
Jisoo, who used to be small, has grown this much now.
The sentence indicates a past state of affairs that hasn't continued to the present.


(1) 지난 번에 만났던 카페에서 또 만날까?
Shall we meet at the cafe we met last time?
The sentence indicates that the action was already done in the past and does not continue now.
