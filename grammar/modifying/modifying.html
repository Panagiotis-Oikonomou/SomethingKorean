Basic Modifying Form

Description:

The modifying form is used to change a verb or an adjective into a noun.

Youtube link
Structure:
Present Tense:
(1)Verb stem-는
(2)Adjective stem-ㄴ/은

(3)N + 이/가 있다, 없다  ⇒  -는

(4)Irregular Verbs  ⇒  -는

Past Tense:
(5)Verb stem-ㄴ/은, -던

Future Tense:
(6)Verb stem-ㄹ/을

Examples:
(1)아마도 먹는 사람.
A person who eats anything.

(1)내가 너를 좋아하는 이유.
The reason i like you.

(2)행복한 여자.
A happy woman.

(2)작은 아기.
A small baby.

(2)아름다운 꽃.
A beautiful flower.
If it has an ㅂ 받침 then you delete the ㅂ and replace it with 운

긴 머리.
Long hair.
If it has an ㄹ 받침 then you delete the ㄹ and replace it with ㄴ

재미있는 책.
An interesting book.

(3)차가 있는 사람.
A person who has a car.

(3)끝이 없는 게임.
A game that has no end.

(4)우는 아기.
A crying baby.

(4)한국에 사는 외국인.
Foreigners who live in korea.

(5)그 영화를 본 사람.
The person who saw the movie.

(5)차가 있던 사람.
The person who had a car.
With words like 있다 or 없다 in Past Tense, you do not use ㄴ/은 but 던 or 았던/었던
(6)먹을 사람.
The person who will eat.

(6)공부할 사람. The person who will study.
