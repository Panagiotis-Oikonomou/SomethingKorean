B. From the time adverbs
(1) 엄마가 이제 읽던 책.
From the adverb 이제 you know "읽던" started yesterday. So it cannot mean "regularly" the only possible meaning would have to be the "the book that mom started reading yesterday but hasn't finished yet".


(3) 이거 동생이 쓰던 노트북이네. 요즘도 쓰나?
