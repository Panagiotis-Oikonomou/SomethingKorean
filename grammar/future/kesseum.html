Future Tense 1

Youtube link
Structure:
Verb/Adjective stem + 겠다/겠습니다

Examples:
1 and 2 the -겠습니다 is used more but in 3 -겠어 is better.
1. Firm will or Determination (subject I)
내일부터 술을 안 마시겠습니다.
I will not drink starting from tomorrow.

담배를 끊겠습니다.
I will stop smoking.
2. Conveying information that something is about to happen (usually in in formal situations)
비행이가 곧 착륙하겠습니다.
The plane will land soon.
3. Guessing (it must be~)
그만 좀 하세요. 아기가 울겠어요.
Please stop. The baby might cry.

요즘 책을 쓰고 있죠? 바쁘시겠어요.
You write a book these days right? You must be very busy.
4. 겠어요?(Rhetorical question of something that's obviously not true)
A: 지수씨 배 안 고파요?
     Jisoo are you not hungry?
B: 아까 점심을 그렇게 많이 먹었는데 배가 고프겠어?
     I had that much lunch but do you really think I would be hungry?

A: 지수가 제 선물을 안 받을 까봐 걱정돼요.
     I worry that Jisoo might not take the gift.
B: 이렇게 좋은 선물을 누가 받겠어요?
     Who wouldn't receive such a good gift?
5. Question: Would you? (in a polite way)
주문하시겠어요.
Would you order?

어디 가시겠어요.
Where would you like to go?
