Future Tense 3

Youtube link
Structure:
Verb stem-ㄹ/을 래

Examples:
1 is often interchangeable with verb + ㄹ/을 거예 (When the subject is I or we)
1. Informing your choice/decision (Subject: I, not so serious)
나는 안 갈래요.
I won't go (it's your decision).

오늘 저녁 안 먹을래요.
I won't eat dinner today.
2. Invitation or Suggestion, (Would you like to/Do you want to verb?) "Subject: you"
영화 볼래요?
Do you want to watch a movie?

오늘은 집에서 쉴래요?
Do you want to take a rest at home today?
