Future Tense 2

Youtube link
Structure:
Verb stem-ㄹ/을 게 (Subject: I)

Examples:
1. Making a Promise
내일 전화할게요.
I'll call you tomorrow.

이제부터 안 그럴게요.
I won't be like that from now on.
2. Informing someone what I'll do, usually right now or near future, to sound considerate
잠시만 화징실 좀 다녀올게요.
I will go to the bathroom for a while.

지금 너무 피곤해서 좀 쉴게요. 이따가 얘기하자.
I'm really tired now so I'm sorry. I will be sure to finish it tomorrow.
