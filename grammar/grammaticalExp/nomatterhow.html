No matter how much you Verb/Adjective

Description:

This structure is used to show that no matter the effort or situation, the result doesn't change.

Youtube link
Structure:
아무리 + Verb/Adjective-아/어도

Examples:
아무리 공부해도 시험을 못 봐요.
No matter how much I study, I do badly on the test.

아무리 먹어도 배가 안 불러요.
No matter how much I eat, I don't feel full.

아무리 비싸도 사고 싶어요.
Even if it's expensive, I want to buy it.

아무리 똑똑해도 매일 연습하지 않으면 한국어를 잘 할 수 없어요.
No matter how smart you are if you don't practice everyday you can't be good at Korean.
