I Should Verb and I Have to Verb

Description:

The first grammar point expresses the speaker's realization or intention to do something based on the current situation or reflection it is someting like "I should" or "I guess I have to". The second grammar point describes a general obligation, rule, or requirement it can be used to mean "I have to".

Youtube link
Structure:
(1)Verb-아/어 야겠다 / 야되겠다
(2)Verb-아/어 + 야 되다 / 야 하다

Examples:
(1)냄새가 심해서 환기를 해야겠어요.
It smells bad, so I should ventilate.

(2)학생은 숙제를 해야 해요.
Students have to do homework.

(2)운전할 때 조심해야 돼요.
You have to be careful when driving.
