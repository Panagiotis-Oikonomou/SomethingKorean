Adverbial Clause

Description:

This grammar point is used to express the time or occasion when an action or event happens. It's similar to saying "when" or "while" in English, and it's used to indicate a specific time or moment during which something occurs.

Structure:
Verb stem-ㄹ/을 때

Examples:
영화를 볼 때행복했어요.
I was happy when I watched the movie.

운동 할 때마시고 싶어요.
I want to drink when I exercise.

학교에 갈 때 만나자요.
Let's meet when I go to school.
