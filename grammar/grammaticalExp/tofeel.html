To Feel someway

Description:

This grammar point is used to express that something feels a certain way or that you feel a particular emotion or impression based on an adjective. Using a verb is used to express that you feel a certain way about an action or get the feeling that something is happening or will happen.

Youtube link
Structure:
Verb stem-는 마음이 들다
Adjective stem-ㄴ/은 마음이 들다

Examples:
슬픈 마음이 들어요.
I feel sad.

그 사람은 항상 친절한 마음이 들어요.
I feel that person is always kind.

그는 자꾸 나를 도와주는 마음이 들어요.
I feel like he keeps helping me.

후회하는 마음이 들어요.
I feel regretful.
