Comparative expression

Description:

This grammar point is used to indicate a situation where the more something happens, the more something else is true or happens. It's used to express a correlation where one action or condition intensifies another. It translates roughly as "the more (verb), the more (something else)".

Structure:
Verb stem-ㄹ/을 수록

Examples:
공부할수록 실력이 늘어요.
The more I study, the more my skills improve.

이 영화를 볼수록 재미있어요.
The more I watch this movie, the more interesting it becomes.

시간이 갈수록 먹고 싶어요.
The more the time passes, the more I want to eat.
