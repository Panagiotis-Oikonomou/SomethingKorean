Assuming a hypothetical situation

Description:

This expression is a hypothetical conditional, often used to speculate about situations that aren't real yet, or are imagined.

Youtube link
Structure:
(만약) Verb stem-ㄴ/는다면
(만약) Adjective stem + 다면

Examples:
(만약) 두 개중 하나만 골라야 한다면요?
What if you have to choose only one among two choices?

(만약) 모르는 사람이 말을 건다면요?
What if a stranger comes to talk to you?

(만약) 똑똑하다면요?
What if you are not smart?
