Liking something

Description:

This grammar point means "to like" or "to be pleased with" something. It expresses that something is to your liking or that you find something appealing or enjoyable.

Youtube link
Structure:
Noun-이/가 마음에 들다

Examples:
이 영화가 마음에 들어요.
I like this movie.

저는 그 노래가 마음에 들어요.
I like that song.

이 옷이 마음에 들어요.
I like this outfit.
