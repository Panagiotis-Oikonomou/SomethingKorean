To Verb without a Noun

Description:

This grammar point negates the presence or use of something when doing the action that follows.

Youtube link
Structure:
Noun + 없이 + Negative Verb

Examples:
돈 없이 살 수 없어요.
I can't live without money.

너 없이 재미없어.
I'm boring without you.

허락 없이 들어오지 마세요.
Don't come in without permission.
