V/A-아/어서

Youtube link
Structure:
Verb/Adjective-아/어 + 서
You can use the present tense or the past tense, but you don't write it like this 아팠어서.

Examples:
A: 왜 성적이 좋아요?
     Why is your grade so good?
B: 공부를 열심히 해서요.
      Because I study hard.

집에 늦게 와서 안 먹었어요.
Because I came home late, I didn't eat.
