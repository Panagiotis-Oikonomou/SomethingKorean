-처럼, -같은 particles

Description:

-처럼 is used when comparing actions or qualities and is often placed after a noun. It describes how something is (similar in nature or quality) to something else.

-같은 is used to describe things or people that are similar or identical to something else. It can be translated as "the same as", "similar to", or "like" in some contexts. -같은 can be used after a noun to indicate that something or someone is of the same kind or category as another.

-처럼 focuses more on comparing qualities or actions (e.g., "like" in terms of appearance, behavior, or characteristics). -같은 is used to show that something is exactly the same or similar in terms of category or class.

Examples:
그는 고양이처럼 귀여워요.
He is cute like a cat.

이 영화는 제가 전에 본 영화와 같은 종류예요.
This movie is the same type as the one I saw before.
