-밖에 particle

Description:

-밖에 is used to indicate a limitation or restriction, meaning "only" or "nothing but". It often implies that there is nothing else or nothing more than what is being mentioned.

You need to use -밖에 only with negative verbs.
Examples:
돈이 10,000원밖에 없어요.
I have only 10,000 won.

한국어밖에 못 해요.
I can only speak Korean.

그 영화밖에 안 봤어요.
I only watched that movie.
