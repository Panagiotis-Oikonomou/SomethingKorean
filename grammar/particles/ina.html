-(이)나 particle

Description:

-(이)나 is used to mean "or", "or something", "at least" or "even".

Examples:
책이나 신문을 읽을 거예요.
I'll read a book or a newspaper.

저는 집에 가서 밥이나 먹을 거예요.
I am going to go home and eat rice or something.

사과나 하나 주세요.
Please give me at least one apple.

이것은 5,000원이나 해요.
This costs as much as 5,000 won.
