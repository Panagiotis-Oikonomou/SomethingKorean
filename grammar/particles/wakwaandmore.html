-와/과, -(이)랑, -하고 particles

Description:

-와/과 are the most formal and standard connectors for "and" when linking nouns. The particles are used in formal contexts or when writing (news articles, formal writing). -(이)랑 is more casual and conversational compared to -와/과.It's commonly used in everyday speech, especially among friends or family. -하고 is also informal but not as casual as -(이)랑. It's commonly used in both spoken and written language. It can also mean "with" depending on the context.

-와 is used after a noun that ends in a vowel.
-과 is used after a noun that ends in a consonant.

Examples:
제프와 지수는 올 거예요.
Jeff and Jisoo will come.

제프랑 지수는 올 거예요.
Jeff and Jisoo will come.

친구하고 영화 봤어요.
I watched a movie with a friend.
