-도 particle

Description:

-도 is used to mean "also", "too", or "even". It's used to indicate that something applies in addition to something else or to emphasize that something is included or involved in a certain situation.

Examples:
저도 가고 싶어요.
I want to go too.

그 사람은 한국어도 잘해요.
That person speaks Korean well too.

그는 학생도 아니에요.
He is not even a student.
