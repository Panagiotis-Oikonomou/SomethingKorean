Verb/Adjective-기

Description:

In Korean, the "Verb + -기" construction is one of the most common ways to turn a verb into a noun-like form. It takes the action described by the verb and treats it like a "thing" or "concept" rather than an action you're doing right now.

Youtube link
Examples:
한국어 배우기 어려워요.
Learning Korean is difficult.

외국인이 한국어를 혼자 공부하기 어려워요.
Foreigners studying korean alone is difficult.

저는 읽기를 잘하는데 듣기를 못해요.
I am good at reading but I am not good at listening.
