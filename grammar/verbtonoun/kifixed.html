Verb/Adjective-기 Fixed Expressions

Description:

Some expressions are frozen with -기. If you use -는 것, it would change the nuance or meaning of the expression.

Youtube link
1. -기(가) 좋다(It's good to Verb, It suits for doing Verb)

여기는 수영하기가 좋아요.
It's good for swimming here.

2. -기(가) 쉽다(It's likely to Verb)

언어가 다르면 사람들이 오해하기 쉬워어요.
If a language is different people are likely to misunderstand you.

3. -기(가) 힘들다/어렵다(It's unlikely to Verb)

길에서 연예인을 보기 힘들어요.
It's unlikely to see a celebrity on the street.

4. 기(를) 바래다(To hope)

여러분의 꿈이 이루어지기를 바래요.
I hope your dreams come true.

5. -기로 결정하다(Decide to Verb)

저는 매일 공부하기로 결정했어요.
I decided to study every day.

6. -기로 약속하다(Promise to Verb)

선생님과 공부를 열심히 하기로 약속했어요.
I promised to study hard with the teacher.

7. -기 시작하다(Begin to Verb)

아이들이 떠들기 시작했어요.
The children begin to be loud.
