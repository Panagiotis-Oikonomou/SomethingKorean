Difference between -기 and -는 것

Youtube link
Description:

-기 treats the action as an abstract act/event/state, no focus on who/when/how specifically, like saying "doing" in English as an activity name.

-는 것 treats the action as a thing/fact/process in reality, like saying "the fact that (someone) does" or "the thing that (someone) is doing"

책 읽기는 재미있어요.
Reading books is fun.
Reading in general, as an activity.

책을 읽는 것은 재미있어요.
The act of reading books is fun.
More descriptive, could be about now or a specific case.
