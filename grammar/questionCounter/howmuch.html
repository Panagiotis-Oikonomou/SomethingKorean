얼마나 & 얼마

Description:

얼마 is used to ask how much is the price of an object

얼마나 is used to ask how much of an adjective someone or something is

Youtube Link
Examples:
이거 전부 얼마예요/얼마해요?
How much are all of this?

저 얼마나 예뻐요?
저 얼만큼 예뻐요?
How pretty am I?

A: 한국 여름은 얼마나 더워요?
     How hot is Korean summer?
B: 동남 아시아만큼 더워요.
     Is as hot as SouthEast Asia.

Description:

How much verb have you done.

Structure:
얼마나 + (adverb) + Verb

Examples:
공부를 얼마나 (오래) 했어요?
How long did you study?

숙제를 얼마나 했어요?
How much did you study?
i. 다 했어요.
   I finished it.

ii. 거의 다 했어요.
   I am almost finished.

iii. 많이 했어요.
   I did a lot.

iv. 얼마 안 했어요. / 별로 안 했어요.
   I haven't finished much.

v. 하나도 안 했어요.
   I haven't done anything.
