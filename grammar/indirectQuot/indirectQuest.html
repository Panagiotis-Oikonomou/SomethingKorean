Indirect Quotation for Questions

Youtube link
Structure:

Present tense:
Verb/Adjective stem + 냐고 묻다, 물어보다

Past tense:
Verb/Adjective/이다-았/었냐고 묻다, 물어보다

Fututre tense:
Verb/Adjective/이다-ㄹ/을 거냐고 묻다, 물어보다

Examples:
지수가 한국인이냐고 물어봤어요.
I asked if Jisoo is Korean.

친구한테 왜 집이 안 갔냐고 물어봤어요.
I asked my friend why he didn't go home.

지수한테 뭘 마실 거냐고 물어봐요.
I asked Jisoo what she would drink.

ㄴ/은지 indirect question vs 냐고
The first one can be followed by many other vers like: 알다, 모르다, 생각하다, 기억하다, etc.
The second one is mostly followed by 묻다, 물어보다
