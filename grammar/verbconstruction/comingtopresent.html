Action continuing from the past

Description:

This grammar point conveys the idea of action continuing from the past to the present or movement toward the speaker while performing an action. It';s often used when talking about actions or events that have been happening over time and up to the present moment.

Structure:
Verb-아/어 오다

Examples:
지금까지 잘 살아왔어요.
I have been living well so far.

사장님이 회사를 잘 운영해왔어요.
The boss has been managing the company well.
