Completion

Description:

This grammar point is used to indicate completing or finishing an action, often with the nuance of something being done completely or irreversibly. It can also convey a sense of regret, relief or unexpected results depending on the context.

Structure:
Verb-아/어 버리다

Examples:
빚을 다 갚아버렸어요.
I paid off all my debt. (you feel relief about it)

음식을 다 먹어버렸어요.
I ate all the food. (you feel regret about it).
