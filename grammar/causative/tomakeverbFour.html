To make someone verb(게 해주다)

Description:

This grammar pattern is used to express the idea of helping someone to do something (indicates direct helpful effect), it is interchangeable with -게 도와주다 but indicates less proactive help in comparison. It can also be used to give permission from the causer to the causee or to use it when making a wish (-게 주세요).

Structure:
Verb/Adjective stem + 게 주다

Examples:
이 책은 너무 재미있어서 잠을 깨게 해줬어요.
This book is so fun that it (directly helps with=) wakes me up.

오늘 일을 다 끝내서 사장님이 제가 일찍 퇴근하게 해줬어요.
I finished today's work, so my boss let me get off work early.

꿈이 잘 보게 해주세요.
Please make my dream come true.
