To make someone verb(게 만들다)

Description:

This grammar pattern is the same with -게 하다 but when it's used with a verb, it adds the nuance of forcing somebody/something being bound to happen inevitable.

Youtube link 1  Youtube link
Structure:
Verb/Adjective stem + 게 만들다

Examples:
선생님께서 학생들을 집에 가게 만들셨어요.
The teacher "forced" the students to go home.

나는 그 사람이 나를 사랑하게 만들 거예요.
I will make him fall in love with me.

이 책은 저를 열심히 공부하게 만들어요.
This book cannot but make me study hard.
