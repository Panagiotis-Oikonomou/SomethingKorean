Adding suffixes To Transitive verbs

Description:

Causative verbs are used to indicate that the subject makes or lets someone do something. You actually do the action.

Youtube link
Structure:
Transitive Verb/Adjective + 이/리/히/기/우/추/구/시/애
It has the same rule as it is in the Passive

Examples:
1. 먹다 ≭ 먹이다(to feed)
고양이는 생선이나 닭을 먹어요.
The cat eats fish or chicken.

저는 고양이를 생선이나 닭을 먹여요.
I feed my cat fish or chicken.
It is often used with 한테/에게, only it it is a person or animal
2. 입다 ≭ 입히다(to dress somebody)
제가 제 딸에게 예쁜 드레스를 입혔어요.
I dressed my daughter a pretty dress.
3. 풀다 ≭ 풀리다(to make solve)
선생님께서 학생들한테 단어 퀴즈를 풀렸어요.
The teacher made the students do a vocabulary quiz.
4. 안다 ≭ 안기다(to make hug/hold)
제 남편이 아기를 안고 있어요.
My husband is holding the baby.

제가 제 남편한테 아기를 안겼어요.
I left my husband hold the baby.
5. 타다(to ride, get in a vehicle) ≭ 태우다(to make ride, to give a ride)
차를 타요. : To ride a car(you use the car as a means of transportation).

차에 타요. : To get in the car.

차를 태워요. : To make somebody to use a car.

차에 태워요. : To make somebody to get in a car.
6. 읽다 ≭ 읽히다(to make read, to read somebody sth)
많은 사람들에게 읽히는 책을 베스트셀러라고 해요.
The book that is read by many people is called a bestseller.
