Adding suffixes To Intransitive verbs

Description:

Causative verbs are used to indicate that the subject makes or lets someone do something. You actually do the action.

Youtube link
Structure:
Intransitive Verb/Adjective + 이/리/히/기/우/추/구/시/애
It has the same rule as it is in the Passive

Examples:
1. 늘다(to increase, improve) ≭ 늘리다(to make increase/improve, to increase/improve sth)
최곤에 혼자 사는 사람이 늘었어요.
Recently the number of people living alone has increased.

저는 한국어 실력을 늘리고 싶어요.
I want to improve my Korean skills.
2. 웃다 ≭ 웃기다(to make laugh)
아빠는 엄마를 자주 웃겨요.
My dad makes my mom laugh frequently.
3. 앉다 ≭ 앉히다
손님들이 소파에 앉았어요.
The guests sat down in the sofa.

저는 손님들을 소파에 앉혔어요.
I sat the guests on the couch.
4. 살다 ≭ 살리다(to make live/survive, to save, to rescue)
의사 선생님 덕분에 친구가 살았어요.
My friend survived thanks to the doctor.

의사 선생님께서 저를 살려줬어요.
The doctor saves my life.
