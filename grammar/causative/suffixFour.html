Adding suffixes extra

Description:

Causative verbs are used to indicate that the subject makes or lets someone do something. You actually do the action.

Youtube link
Structure:
Verb/Adjective + 이/리/히/기/우/추/구/시/애
It has the same rule as it is in the Passive

Examples:
1. 속다(to be deceived/fooled) ≭ 속이다(to deceive/fool)
만우절 거짓말에 속았어요.
I was fooled by April fools day.

다른 사람을 속이는 말을 거짓말이라고 해요.
Lies are the words that you use to deceive other people.
2. 붙다(to get attached/stick) ≭ 붙이다(to attach sth)
머리에 껌이 붙었어요.
A gum it's stuck to my hair.

운전을 처음 하는 사람들은 "초보 운전" 스티커를 차 뒤에 붙여요.
People that drive for the first time attach a sticker in the back of their car.
3. 익다(to be cooked) ≭ 익히다(to cook sth, to make sth cooked)
감자가 아직 안 익었으니까 조금만 기다리세요.
The potato is not yet cooked so please wait a little bit.

닭고기를 익히지 않으면 배탈이 날 거예요.
If you don't cook the chicken you will get stomach ash.
4. 옮다(to be transferred/infected) ≭ 옮기다(to transfer/transmit/infect sth)
친구한테 감기가 옮았어요.
I caught a cold from my friend.

핸드폰에 있는 사진을 컴퓨터를 옮기고 싶어요.
I want to transfer the photos from my phone to my desktop.
5. 남다(to remain, to be left) ≭ 남기다(to leave behind)
밤이 왜 이렇게 많이 남았어요.
Why is there so much meal left.

배가 불어서 밥을 많이 남겼어요.
I am full so I left behind a lot of food.
6. 없다 ≭ 없애다(to remove/get rid of)
커피를 쏟아서 바지에 얼룩이 졌어요. 얼룩을 없앨 수 있는 방법을 알려주세요.
I spill my coffee so theres a stain in my pants. Please let me know a way to get rid of the stain.
7. 맡다(to take on/undertake) ≭ 맡기다(let someone undertake, to entrust)
제가 이번 프로젝트를 맡았어요.
I undertook that project.

대표님께서 저에게 중요한 일을 맡겨 주셔서 영광입니다.
I am honored that the CEO entrusted me with an important task.
8. 끼다(to wear on your-ears/eyes/fingers) ≭ 끼우다(to put it on for someone)
도서관에서 음악을 듣고 싶으면 반드시 이어폰을 끼어주세요.
If you want to listen music in the library wear earphones.

여자친구에게 청혼하면서 반지를 끼워요.
I put the ring on my girlfriend's finger while proposing.
