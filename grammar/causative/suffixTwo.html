Adding suffixes Descriptive verbs

Description:

Causative verbs are used to indicate that the subject makes or lets someone do something. You actually do the action.

Youtube link
Structure:
Descriptive Verb/Adjective + 이/리/히/기/우/추/구/시/애
It has the same rule as it is in the Passive

Examples:
1. 넓다(to be wide/spacious) ≭ 넓히다(to widen)
방이 넓어요.
The room is spacious.

외국어 공부는 제 사야를 넓혔어요.
Studying a foreign language widened my horizons.
2. 좁다(to be narrow/unspacious) ≭ 좁히다(to narrow sth down)
방이 너무 좁아요.
The room is too narrow.

외견 차이를 좁히려면 대화가 필요해요.
To narrow the gap of opinions, conversation is necessary.
3. 늦다 ≭ 늦추다(to postpone/push back)
늦어서 미안해요.
I'm sorry for being late.

회의 시간을 한 시간 늦출 수 있을까요?
Can we push back our meeting time by an hour?
