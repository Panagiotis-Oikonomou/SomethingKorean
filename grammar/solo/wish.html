Expressing ones Wish

Description:

This grammar point is a polite way to express a wish, suggestion, or desire for something to happen, similar to saying "I would appreciate it if" or "It would be good if"

Youtube link
Structure:
Verb stem-(으)면 좋겠습니다

Examples:
가면 좋겠습니다.
I hope I can go.

한국어를 잘하면 좋겠습니다.
I wish I get good at Korean.

먹으면 좋겠습니다.
It would be good if I eat.
