Concern

Description:

This grammar point expresses concern or fear about a possible future situation or outcome. It's often used when imagining possible negative outcomes.

Structure:
Verb stem-ㄹ/을까봐 걱정되다

Examples:
비가 올까봐 걱정돼요.
I'm worried it might rain

사람들이 나를 이상하게 볼까봐 걱정돼.
I'm worried people might think I'm weird.

늦을까봐 걱정됐어요.
I was worried I might be late.
