Extra Anything

Youtube link
Structure:
A. Question Word + 든지= -ever

B. Verb stem + 든지
1. or (listing possible choices) = -거나
2. whether (usually in the context of something not being important)

뭐든지: whatever, anything
어디든지: wherever, anywhere
언제든지: whenever, anytime
누구든지: whoever, anyone
어떻게든지: however, anyhow
With -거나 it's like "I don't really care", but with -든지 is more polite.

Examples:
뭐든지 할 수 있어요.
I can do anything.

(1)휴일에는 집에서 쉬든지 밖에서 친구들이랑 놀아요.
On holidays, I get some rest at home or hung out with my friends outside.

(2)내일 비가 오든지 회의는 그대로 진행될 거예요.
Whether it rains tomorrow or not the meeting will still be hold.
