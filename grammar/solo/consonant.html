Consonant Assimilation

Description:

Consonant assimilation occurs when a consonant changes its sound to become more like a neighboring consonant. This is common in Korean, especially with 받침 (final consonants).

  • ㄱ 받침 + ㄹ = ㅇ + ㄴ

    • 격리 = 경니
