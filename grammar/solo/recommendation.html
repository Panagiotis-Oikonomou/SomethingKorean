Suggestion, gentle Recommendation

Description:

This grammar pattern is used to make suggestions or gentle recommendations, somewhat like saying: "Why don't you?", "How about?". It carries a friendly, soft, and conversational tone, and it's commonly used when giving advice or proposing an idea.

Youtube link
Structure:
Verb stem + 지 그래

Examples:
너무 피곤해 보여. 좀 쉬지 그래요?
You look really tired. Why don't you take a rest?

그 영화 재미있대. 같이 보지 그래요?
They say that movie is fun. Why don't we watch it together?

배고프면 뭐 좀 먹지 그래요?
If you're hungry, why don't you eat something?
