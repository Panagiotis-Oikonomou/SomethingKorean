Hypothetical

Description:

This grammar pattern is used to talk about hypothetical situations, or concessions—where even if something happens or is true, the result or action will remain unchanged

Youtube link
Structure:
Verb stem-더라도

Examples:
돈이 없더라도 행복할 수 있어요.
Even if you don't have money, you can still be happy.

다시 태어나더라도 널 사랑할 거예요.
Even if I were born again, I would still love you.

혼자 가더라도 여행을 포기하지 않을 거예요.
Even if I have to go alone, I won't give up on the trip.
