Giving and Asking Permission/Prohibition

Description:

-도 돼 is used to express permission or asking for permission. -도 안 돼 is used to express prohibition or that it doesn't work even if you verb. -(으)면 돼 is used to express that something is enough or is all you need to do in order to achieve a particular result or solve a problem. -(으)면 안 돼 is used to express prohibition or to say that something is not allowed or isn't okay to do.

-(으)면 안 돼 is used for plead, suggestion (we)
-도 돼 is used to ask something that you are really curious if that's allowed or not

Structure:
Verb-아/어 + 도 돼
Verb-아/어 + 도 안 돼
Verb stem + (으)면 돼
Verb stem + (으)면 안 돼

Examples:
공부를 많이 안 해도 돼요.
It's okay not to study too much.

엄마, 오늘 조금 늦게 자도 돼요?
Mom is it okay for me to sleep a little late today?

이 컴퓨터는 고쳐봐도 안 돼요.
As for this computer even if I try to fix it, it doesn't work.

저희 동네에서는 밤에 떠들어도 안 돼요.
It's not allowed to raise an animal in my neighborhood.

그 고객한테 내일까지 이매일을 보내시면 돼요.
You can send that customer an email by tomorrow.

오늘 피자 먹으면 안 돼요?
Can I eat pizza today?

늦게 오면 안 돼요.
You should not come late.
