As you...

Description:

This grammar point means "as / like", and the verb before it is usually in honorific or plain form depending on the subject, and it's specific verbs.

Structure:
알다시피  ⇒  "As you know"
보다시피  ⇒  "As you can see"
들으시피  ⇒  "As you heard"
생각하시피  ⇒  "As you think / As you may be thinking"

Examples:
보다시피 날씨가 정말 좋아요.
He came into the room with his shoes still on.

생각하시피 이 일은 쉬운 일이 아네에요.
As you (may) think, this is not an easy task.

들으시피이번 프로젝트는 성공적이었어요.
As you heard, this project was a success.
