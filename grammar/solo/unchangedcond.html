An unchanged condition

Description:

This grammar pattern is used when one action happens while another state or condition remains unchanged. Indicates that a certain state or action is maintained, while another action happens.

Structure:
Verb stem-ㄴ/은 채

Examples:
신발을 신은 채 방에 들어왔어요.
He came into the room with his shoes still on.

옷을 입은 채 수영장에 뛰어들었어요.
He jumped into the pool with his clothes still on.

불을 켠 채로 나갔어요.
I went out leaving the lights on.
