How to say Anything, Anybody, Nothing, Nobody

Youtube link
Structure:
(1)Any Noun: 아무 + Noun
(2)Any Noun (in a sentence): 아무 + Noun + (이)나
(3)Anything: 아무거나
(4)Anyone/Anybody: 아무나
(5)Nothing: 아무것도 + negative verb
(6)No one/Nobody: 아무도 + negative verb
(7)아무거나/아무나 + negative verb

Examples:
(2)아무옷이나 입어도 돼요.
You can wear any clothes.

(3)아무거나 마실 수 있어요.
I can drink anything.

(4)아무나 한국어를 배울 수 있어요.
Anyone can learn Korean.

(5)엄마가 아무것도 안 줬어요.
I didn't get anything from my mom.

(5)아무걱정도없어요. / 안 해 요.
I don't have any worries.

(6)아무도 초대하지 않았어요.
I didn't invite anyone.

(7)저는 아무노래나 안 들어요.
I don't listen to any songs.

(7)아무거나 사지 마세요. 필요한 곳만 사세요.
Don't buy just anything. Only buy what you need.
