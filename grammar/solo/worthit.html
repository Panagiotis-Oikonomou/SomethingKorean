Worth it

Description:

This grammar pattern is used to express that something is worth doing or deserves to be done. It often implies moderate praise — e.g., not amazing, but good enough to try. Often used in recommendations or reviews.

Youtube link
Structure:
Verb-ㄹ/을 만하다

Examples:
이 영화는 정말 볼 만해요.
This movie is really worth watching.

이 식당은 한 번 가볼 만해요.
This restaurant is worth visiting at least once.

이 정도 상처는 참을 만해요.
This level of pain is bearable.
