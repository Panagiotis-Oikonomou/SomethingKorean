Exaggeration

Description:

This grammar pattern is an exaggerated way to express strong emotion about something, typically negative feelings — though not always fatal or serious.

Structure:
Verb-아/어 죽겠다/죽겠네

Examples:
배고파 죽겠다.
I'm so hungry I could die.

아파 죽겠어.
It hurts to death. (It really hurts)
