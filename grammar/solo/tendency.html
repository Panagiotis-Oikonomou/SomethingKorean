Tendency

Description:

This grammar pattern is used to express tendency. You're not saying something is absolutely a certain way, but that it's generally or relatively like that. It's based on the speaker's perception.

Youtube link
Structure:
(1)Verb stem-는 편이다
(1)Adjective stem-은/ㄴ 편이다

Examples:
운동을 자주 하는 편이에요.
I tend to exercise often.(I'm not a total gym rat, but I work out more than average.)

일을 빨리 끝내는 편이에요.
I tend to finish work quickly.

저는 부끄러움을 잘 타는 편이에요.
I am getting shy really easily.(It's not always like that but it happens a lot)

이 음식은 매운 편이에요.
This food is rather spicy.(Maybe not the spiciest, but spicier than average.)
