Limitation, Exclusiveness

Description:

This grammar pattern emphasizes limitation or exclusiveness, that there is nothing else except that one thing. It minimizes, undermines a certain situation/quality or to humbly expresess your own situation/ability.

Youtube link
Structure:
Noun-뿐이다
Verb-ㄹ/을 뿐이다

Examples:
지금은 기다릴 뿐이에요.
Right now, all we can do is wait.

돈이 없어서 구경만 할 뿐이에요.
I have no money, so I'm just looking around.

집에 저뿐이에요.
There is only me at home.
