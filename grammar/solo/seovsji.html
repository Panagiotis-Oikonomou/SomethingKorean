겠어 vs 겠지

Description:

-겠어 can mean assumption, intention or prediction. On the other hand -겠지 can mean soft assumption or guessing.


Examples:
내일 비가 오겠어요.
It looks like it will rain tomorrow. (prediction)

내일 비가 오겠지.
I guess it'll rain tomorrow. (soft prediction)

배가 고프겠어요.
You must be hungry. (assumption)

이제 그만해야겠어요.
I should stop now. (intention/decision)
