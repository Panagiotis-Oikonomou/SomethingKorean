Compare bad choices

Description:

This grammar pattern shows a choice between two undesirable (or less preferred) actions, and emphasizes that the second option is better (or less bad) than the first.

Youtube link
Structure:

Verb stem-느니 차라리 + Verb

Examples:
혼자 있느니 차라리 집에 갈게요.
Rather than staying alone, I'd rather go home.

실패하느니 차라리 시도하지 않겠어요.
Rather than failing, I'd rather not try at all.

담배를 끊느니 차라리 죽겠어요.
I prefer to die than to quit smoking.
